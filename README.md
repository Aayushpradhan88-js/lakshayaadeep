## Overview

Lakshaydeep.com is a web platform designed to showcase projects, event, and events, while enabling donations and volunteer engagement.

The platform supports Lakshaydeep’s mission of improving quality of life for people of all age groups — from youth (18–30) to elderly (40–80+).

---

## Why Does This NGO Exist?

Lakshaydeep exists to address environmental degradation and promote a sustainable future in Nepal.

With increasing pollution, deforestation, and lack of environmental awareness, there is a growing need for organized efforts to restore and protect nature.

Lakshaydeep focuses on planting trees, running awareness event, and encouraging community participation to build a greener and healthier Nepal.

---

## 🌱 About Lakshaydeep

### Mission

To plant trees across Nepal and contribute towards a greener environment.

### Vision

To make Nepal green, clean, and environmentally sustainable for future generations.

### Target Beneficiaries

- Local communities across Nepal
- Youth and volunteers
- Future generations
- Environment and biodiversity

### Problem They Solve

- Deforestation and environmental degradation
- Lack of awareness about sustainability
- Limited community involvement in ecological activities

## 🎯 Purpose of Lakshaydeep

Lakshaydeep operates through two main approaches:

### 1. Projects

Long-term initiatives (6 months – 1 year or more) focused on sustainable impact such as:

- Tree plantation drives
- Environmental campaigns
- Community development efforts

### 2. event

Short-term activities (1–2 days) aimed at awareness and engagement:

- Local events
- Clean-up campaigns
- Workshops and awareness sessions

## 💡 What Impact Does This Website Create?

This website acts as a **digital bridge** between the NGO and the community.

It helps to:

- Increase awareness about environmental issues
- Encourage people to participate in event and projects
- Simplify donation processes
- Showcase transparency and impact of work
- Build a strong volunteer network

## 👥 Who Benefits?

- Volunteers seeking meaningful engagement
